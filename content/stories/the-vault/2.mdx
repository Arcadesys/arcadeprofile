They found the key in a safety deposit box that had been misfiled for forty years.

It was Dev's idea to check the old records — the ones the bank had boxed up when they converted to digital in the nineties. Three banker's boxes of index cards, each one a tiny biography of someone who'd trusted a steel box with their secrets.

Box 1247 didn't match any slot in the vault. The index card read simply: *Basement. Do not open before 2026.*

"Well," said Maya, turning the key over in her hand. It was warm, which didn't make sense. "It's 2026."
